Welcome to the Beginner's Guide to Free Software

This website helps you get started with free software and GNU/Linux distributions.

About Free Software Foundation

The Free Software Foundation (FSF) is a nonprofit organization that promotes computer user freedom and defends the rights of all free software users.

Our mission is to advocate for free software and ensure that all computer users have the freedom to control their own computing.

  • GIMP - Free image editing software.

  • Kdenlive - Free video editing software.
  • Calibre - E-book management tool.
  • Inkscape - Vector graphic design tool.

Protect Your Privacy

Consider these methods to enhance your privacy:

  • Use privacy-focused browsers like LibreWolf.
  • Consider using encrypted messaging apps like Signal.
  • Use VPNs to mask your IP address and location.
  • Make purchases with cash or cryptocurrencies like Monero to maintain anonymity.
  • Use torrenting tools responsibly and legally.
